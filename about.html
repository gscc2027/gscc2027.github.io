About the GSCC

The Graduate Student Combinatorics Conference (GSCC) is an annual conference organized by and for graduate students. It has the expressed purpose of

  • providing a space for graduate students to present their research in a comfortable and professional setting,
  • bring graduate researchers from across the U.S. together to foster collaboration and progress,
  • and introduce graduate students to new fields of research within combinatorics.
