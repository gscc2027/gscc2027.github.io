Graduate Student Combinatorics Conference 2027

March 21-23 | Georgia Institute of Technology | Atlanta, GA

About the Conference

The Graduate Student Combinatorics Conference (GSCC) is an annual event organized by and for graduate students in combinatorics. Its hosting university rotates each year, and in 2027 GSCC will be held at the Georgia Tech from March 21 to March 23.
