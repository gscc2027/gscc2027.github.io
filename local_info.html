Getting There From ATL

We recommend flying into Atlanta through Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL).

Via MARTA

MARTA is Atlanta's public transportation system. There is a rail station located inside the airport, near the baggage claim area at the north end of the airport. A one-way fare will cost you $2.50. Trains leave approximately every 10 minutes. All trains leaving the airport travel north toward Georgia Tech. The two closest stops to Georgia Tech are North Avenue and Midtown. You can take the GT Stinger bus from Midtown station to campus. Look for the Gold Route at the GT Stinger bus schedule.

Via Rideshare Services

After collecting your bags at baggage claim, you will follow signs to the rideshare pick-up lot. It is about a 5 minute walk to the designated rideshare pickup area. The typical fare is approximately $35.
